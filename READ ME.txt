GALACTIC CONQUEST
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Galactic Conquest is and endless space shooter with randomly
Generating maps hostiles and power up types / locations
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
CONTROLS
W: Move Forward
S: Move Backwards
A: Rotate Left
D: Roatate Right
MOUSE LEFT BUTTON: Primary Fire (Can be held)
MOUSE RIGHT: Secondary Fire (Can be Held)
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
HOSTILES
4 types of hostiles with minor variations in health, speed,
 fire rate and rotation speed
1 Spawner type the randomly spawns one of the 4 types 
every 60 seconds
/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
POWERUP
3 power ups that increases speed, give ammo for secondary, or 
Increase health.
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
WEAPONS
Primary:
 Fires a plasma shot that deals 1 damage to anything it hits
 and can cancel out any hostile plasma shots. Only fired forward
Secondary:
Missile (Starts with 0 ammo) needs ammo to fire. Will track the nearest
Hostile to the missile and when contact is made the missile 
detonate into a miniature Black hole that sucks everything 
near it into itself destroying it, active for a limited amount of
 time. Can be fired in any direction.
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
UI 
Shows health and lives in top left corner. Shows missile ammo in top right
And in top center the number of missions completed from last death. 
On the bottom right shows number of enemy spawners and ships left. Finaly
An arrow around your ship that points in the direction of the closest spawner
/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
4 scenes, a Menu, a Game, a win and a loss scene
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Some audio functionality 
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
MAP generation
All tiles are randomly generated each mission, RED = SPAWNER LOCATION 
And GREEN = POWERUP location. All other colours have no effect. 
////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
